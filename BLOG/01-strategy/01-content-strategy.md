# StewardAI — Content Strategy

> Translating the corporate vision into actionable content pillars, voice, and execution standards.
> **Owner:** Eunice · **Last updated:** 2026-04-27 · **Status:** v1.0

---

## 1. Vision Alignment

**Corporate vision (working statement).** StewardAI exists to make AI automation reliable, transparent, and ROI-positive for the practitioners who actually run the work — marketers, planners, and operators — not just data scientists.

**Brand promise.** *AI automation that ships. Stewarded by you.*

**What we are NOT.** We are not a no-code toy, a model marketplace, or a research lab. We are an opinionated automation platform with built-in governance.

---

## 2. Audience

| Segment | Role | Pain | What they want from our content |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Primary** | Marketing / growth practitioner | "I have 20 ideas, no time to ship them." | Templates, ROI proof, copy-paste workflows |
| **Primary** | Operations / planning lead | "Tools sprawl. Outputs aren't auditable." | Governance patterns, integration playbooks |
| **Secondary** | Founder / Head of GTM | "Where's the ROI math?" | Case studies, benchmarks, ROI calculators |

Tone for all segments: **practitioner-to-practitioner**. Never "thought leadership" detached from the keyboard.

---

## 3. Content Pillars

Four pillars, each tied to a buying-stage and a measurable outcome.

### Pillar 1 — AI Workflow Playbooks
Templated, step-by-step automation recipes (e.g., "Auto-segment new leads in 7 minutes").
- **Buying stage:** evaluation → activation
- **KPI:** template downloads, signups
- **Format:** step-by-step posts + downloadable JSON workflow

### Pillar 2 — Marketing Automation with AI
Function-by-function takes (email, SEO, paid, content ops) on what AI changes *now*.
- **Buying stage:** awareness → evaluation
- **KPI:** organic search traffic, dwell time
- **Format:** long-form SEO articles + comparison tables

### Pillar 3 — AI Stack & Tool Comparisons
Honest reviews, integration guides, ROI calculators.
- **Buying stage:** evaluation → decision
- **KPI:** branded search lift, conversion to demo
- **Format:** evergreen comparison hubs + interactive calculators

### Pillar 4 — AI Governance for Practitioners
Safety, compliance, prompt evals, brand-voice guardrails — translated for non-engineers.
- **Buying stage:** decision → expansion
- **KPI:** enterprise lead quality, sales-assist asset usage
- **Format:** frameworks, checklists, policy templates

---

## 4. Voice & Tone

- **Concrete over conceptual.** Every claim cites a workflow, a number, or a screenshot.
- **Jargon-light.** "AI agent" survives. "Multi-modal RAG-augmented orchestration" does not.
- **Confident, never hype.** We name trade-offs out loud.
- **Practitioner-to-practitioner.** First-person plural ("we shipped this"), second person addressing the reader ("you'll spend ~30 minutes").
- **Bilingual.** EN is canonical; KR is a true localization (idioms, examples, units), not a literal translation.

**Banned phrases:** "revolutionary", "game-changer", "unlock the power of", "in today's fast-paced world".

## 5. Editorial Cadence (first 90 days)

| Week | Pillar | Asset | Channel priority |
|---|---|---|---|
| W1–2 | Launch | Landing page + 3 cornerstone posts | Web, LinkedIn, X |
| W3–4 | Pillar 1 | Workflow playbook #1 (downloadable) | Web, newsletter |
| W5–6 | Pillar 2 | "AI for SEO in 2026" deep-dive | Web, organic search |
| W7–8 | Pillar 3 | StewardAI vs. n8n vs. Zapier (with calculator) | Web, branded search |
| W9–10 | Pillar 1 | Workflow playbook #2 | Web, community |
| W11–12 | Pillar 4 | "Brand-voice guardrails for AI-generated content" | Web, sales enablement |

Target: 2 posts/week (1 cornerstone, 1 supporting) once cadence is established.

---

## 6. Conversion Architecture

Every post must include:
1. A **TL;DR** in the first 80 words (mobile readers scan).
2. One **primary CTA** (book a demo) and one **secondary CTA** (download the workflow JSON or join the newsletter).
3. **Internal links** to at least 2 related posts (topic clusters).
4. A **schema.org Article** block with author, datePublished, dateModified.

## 7. Success Metrics (90-day targets)

- Organic sessions: **0 → 5,000/mo**
- Email subscribers: **0 → 1,200**
- Demo requests from blog: **0 → 60/mo**
- Avg. mobile time-on-page: **≥ 2:30**
- Core Web Vitals (mobile): **all "Good"** on top 10 pages
