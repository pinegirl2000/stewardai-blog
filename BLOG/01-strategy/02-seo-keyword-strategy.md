> **Primary language:** English · **Secondary:** Korean · **Last updated:** 2026-04-27

---

## 1. Strategic Premise

We are **not** competing for the head term "AI" or "artificial intelligence". We compete for **practitioner-intent**, **action-oriented** queries that signal buying readiness. Every cluster ladders up to one of four content pillars.

**North-star query archetype:** *"how do I [marketer task] with AI"* — high commercial intent, low difficulty for an early-stage domain.

## 4. On-Page SEO Rules

Every published page MUST satisfy:

1. **Title tag:** ≤ 60 chars, primary keyword in first 50%.
2. **Meta description:** 140–155 chars, includes primary keyword + value prop + soft CTA.
3. **H1:** Exactly one, contains primary keyword (or close variant).
4. **URL slug:** kebab-case, ≤ 5 words, primary keyword stem.
5. **First 100 words:** primary keyword + answer to the query (TL;DR pattern).
6. **Internal links:** ≥ 2 links to same-cluster pages, ≥ 1 to a different pillar.
7. **Image alt text:** descriptive (not keyword-stuffed); LCP image ≤ 100 KB.
8. **Schema:** `Article` for posts, `FAQPage` where applicable, `Organization` site-wide.
9. **Hreflang:** `en` ↔ `ko-KR` pairs for translated content.
10. **Mobile Core Web Vitals:** LCP < 2.5s, INP < 200ms, CLS < 0.1.

## 6. Link-Building Priorities (first 90 days)

- Guest posts on practitioner publications (Reforge, Demand Curve, MarketingProfs).
- Submit comparison pages to product directories (G2, Capterra, Product Hunt).
- Quote-led PR: 3 data-backed insights/quarter pitched to trade press.
- Korean: 모비인사이드, 디지털인사이트, AI타임스 (게스트 기고 + 인용 인터뷰).

---

## 7. Measurement Cadence

- **Weekly:** GSC impressions/clicks for top 20 queries, indexing status of new posts.
- **Monthly:** Cluster-level rankings, conversion rate by landing page, internal-link audit.
- **Quarterly:** Full content audit (prune, refresh, consolidate). Refresh cornerstone posts every 90 days minimum.
