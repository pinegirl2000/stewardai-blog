Build Your First AI Agent Workflow in 30 Minutes (No Code Required)

A step-by-step playbook to ship a real AI agent workflow tonight — CRM trigger, Slack notification, one governance guardrail.

TL;DR. The Hello-World of practitioner AI: Slack-delivered, CRM-triggered AI nudge that catches stalled deals. 30 minutes start to finish, no code.

The workflow you're about to build

A trigger watches your CRM for an opportunity that hasn't moved stages in 14 days. An agent reads the recent context and writes a 3-line Slack DM to the AE. A brand-voice check stops anything off-brand from posting.

Step 1 — Create the trigger (5 min)

Pick your CRM. Choose "Opportunity stage unchanged for 14 days, status Open". Run daily at 8am local. Test on the next 5 records — do they look like real stuck deals?
